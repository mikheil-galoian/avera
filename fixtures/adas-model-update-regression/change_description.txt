Updated ADAS perception model from v2.4.0 to v2.4.1.

Change: Retrained YOLOv8-based pedestrian detection model on expanded dataset
including additional night and adverse weather scenarios.

Motivation: Improve overall detection accuracy on highway scenarios.

Risk: Retraining on new data distribution may introduce regression on
previously validated safety-critical scenarios, particularly low-visibility
pedestrian detection.

Affected component: adas-perception-module
Affected requirements: REQ-SAFETY-012, REQ-SAFETY-008, REQ-PERF-001
