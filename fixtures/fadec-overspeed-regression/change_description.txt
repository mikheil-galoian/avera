Refactored interrupt handler in overspeed_protection.c to reduce CPU load during
high-frequency engine monitoring cycles. The interrupt latency optimization
introduced a 19 ms delay in the overspeed protection response path, causing
FADEC-REQ-001 (<=50 ms) to be violated at 63.2 ms.

DAL-A requirement. Release blocking. Requires immediate root cause analysis
and re-verification before any further integration.
