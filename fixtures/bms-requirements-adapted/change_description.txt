This workspace demonstrates AVERA operating on an adapted requirements export rather than only on
the canonical fixture requirements.csv source. The upstream export uses a wider ALM-style schema
with fields like `requirement_id`, `title`, `module`, `threshold_signal`, and `threshold_value`.

The export is normalized into the stable AVERA requirements.csv contract before analysis. The
underlying BMS regression remains the same: a fast-charge cooling logic change in
src/bms/thermal_manager.c causes the maximum cell temperature to exceed the allowed threshold.
