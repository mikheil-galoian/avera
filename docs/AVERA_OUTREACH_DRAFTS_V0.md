## 1. Short Email Draft

Subject:

`Evidence-first review for verification and release artifacts`

Body:

`Hi — we built a local workflow called AVERA that turns engineering artifacts like verification logs, JUnit/xUnit reports, simulation CSV, and requirements exports into one stable review path.`

`The key idea is simple: keep the evidence explicit, normalize the upstream artifacts, and support a more trustworthy release-review decision without asking a team to change everything at once.`

`If this is relevant to your workflow, the next step is not a broad rollout. It is one narrow pilot around one artifact family your team already uses.`

## 2. Short DM Draft

`We built a local evidence-first review workflow called AVERA. It can normalize verification artifacts like logs, JUnit/xUnit, and simulation CSV into one stable release-review path. If your team already has a repeated verification review workflow, I’d be glad to show a short 10-minute walkthrough.`  

## 3. Reddit-Style Draft

Title idea:

`We built a local workflow for turning verification artifacts into one release-review path — curious how teams handle this today`

Post draft:

`We’ve been building a local evidence-first workflow called AVERA for automotive engineering review. The narrow problem we care about is not “AI for everything,” but a much simpler one:`

`How do you take change descriptions, baseline/current verification evidence, logs, simulation outputs, and requirement links, and turn them into one review path that a human can actually trust before release?`

`Right now AVERA can normalize artifacts like verification log CSV, JUnit/xUnit XML, simulation CSV, and richer requirements exports into one stable local review flow with traceability, decision support, and an evidence pack for handoff.`

`I’m curious how teams here handle this today: what artifact exports do you actually rely on for regression triage or release review, and where does the trust break down?`

Do not oversell:

- no hosted-platform promises
- no enterprise rollout claims
- no “AI replaces engineering judgment” framing

Keep the ask narrow:

- one workflow
- one artifact family
- one pilot conversation
