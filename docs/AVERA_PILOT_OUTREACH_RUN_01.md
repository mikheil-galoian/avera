## 7. Message Discipline

Lead with:

`AVERA is a local evidence-first review workflow that can normalize external engineering artifacts into one stable release-review path.`

Do not lead with:

- AI transformation language
- platform ambition
- enterprise rollout claims
- compliance claims

## 8. First Contact Questions

Use these in order:

1. what verification artifact do you already export today?
2. where does your current regression triage break down?
3. who owns the release-review decision this would support?
4. would one narrow local-first pilot be realistic for your team?
