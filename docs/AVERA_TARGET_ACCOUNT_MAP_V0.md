# AVERA Target Account Map v0

**Date:** 2 May 2026  
**Purpose:** Define the first realistic external targets for AVERA  
**Scope:** Roles, team types, and company profiles rather than a broad market list

## Best First Roles

Start with people who directly feel verification and release friction:

1. validation lead
2. systems engineer
3. release / quality owner
4. embedded software manager
5. verification tooling owner

## Why These Roles First

- validation lead: owns regression pain and review volume
- systems engineer: cares about requirement impact and evidence completeness
- release / quality owner: cares about trust, handoff, and blocking decisions
- embedded software manager: cares about whether the tool fits real team workflows
- verification tooling owner: cares about artifact shape and pilot feasibility

## Team Types To Prioritize

Best early team shapes:

1. BMS verification teams
2. ADAS validation teams
3. embedded ECU release-review teams
4. system integration teams handling repeated baseline/current comparisons

## Company Profiles To Prioritize

Strong first-fit profiles:

- automotive suppliers with structured verification workflows
- OEM subsystem engineering groups
- teams already exporting simulation, requirements, or verification artifacts
- teams with manual release-review or regression-triage bottlenecks

## Company Profiles To Avoid First

Less useful early targets:

- organizations looking for a broad AI platform story first
- teams with no clear artifact trail
- teams without repeated review workflows
- groups that want hosted deployment before proving workflow value

## Best Early Entry Angles

Lead with one of these:

1. repeated regression triage pain
2. difficulty linking evidence to release decisions
3. inconsistent upstream artifact shapes
4. manual review handoff friction

## First Three Target Conversations

The first three conversations should ideally cover:

1. one BMS-oriented team
2. one ADAS-oriented team
3. one general embedded/release-review team

That gives AVERA signal on:

- domain portability
- artifact-family relevance
- narrow pilot fit

## What We Want To Learn

From each conversation, capture:

1. their role
2. their artifact family
3. their repeated review workflow
4. where trust breaks in current triage
5. whether a narrow local-first pilot feels realistic
